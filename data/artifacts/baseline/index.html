About AgentForge

AgentForge began as an internal experiment to automate repetitive interface design decisions without lowering quality.

Over time, we added specialized agents for layout generation, interaction critique, accessibility review, and production handoff.

Today product, design, and frontend teams use AgentForge to move from ideas to high-quality UI faster.
